National Education System

Cultivating human capital through absolute academic rigour and innovative assessment.

I. The Sovereign State Grid (Free)

The backbone of Sinowan prosperity. State schools provide world-class, free education. The curriculum is a rigorous blend of real-life Chinese STEM and Humanities content. It provides foundational discipline and intense academic depth to guarantee technical proficiency.

II. International Academies (Private)

Fee-paying international schools are explicitly permitted for expatriates and citizens preferring alternative pedagogical styles, maintaining international accreditation while adhering strictly to state security guidelines.

III. Sovereign Assessment Matrix (SAM)

To avoid a dystopian academic environment, we have abolished the stressful Gaokao model. Instead, we combine the best pedagogical practices from around the world to create unique testing methods. By utilising continuous digital coursework logging, practical vivas, and collaborative labs, we assess students comprehensively. We aim to be the best educational system worldwide, producing highly adaptable workers without sacrificing youth mental health.
