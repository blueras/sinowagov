Ministry of Commerce & Technological Innovation

Regulating the private sector, managing Special Economic Zones (SEZs), and driving industrial growth under state guidance. Our economy combines dynamic private enterprise with strategic state oversight — a model we call State-Guided Capitalism.

State-Guided Capitalism & Corporate Oversight

The State Council retains a "Golden Share" (1%) and a mandatory board seat in all major corporations. This ensures private profit generation never diverges from collective national interest.

2025 Performance: GDP growth 6.8% · Export growth 12.3% · 47 new factories · 12,000 jobs created

Special Economic Zones (SEZs)

Three SEZs operate with streamlined regulations and tax incentives: Pearl River Delta Tech Hub (Tier 1), Northern Industrial Corridor (Tier 2), Western Development Zone (emerging). Foreign investment in SEZs reached eK 2.3B in 2025.
