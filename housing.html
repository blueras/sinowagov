Ministry of Housing & Urban Development

Managing land monopolies, urban planning, and residential allocations.

I. 70-Year Leasehold Model

The State retains absolute ownership of all land. Citizens may purchase and trade 70-year residential leaseholds. This balances private investment with government sovereignty.

II. Hukou Residency System

Your residency status determines access to local clinics and schools. Transfers to Tier-1 cities are algorithmically managed to prevent overcrowding.
