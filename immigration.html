Sovereign Citizenship Statutes & Residency Regulations

Clause I: Dual Citizenship Parameters

The People's Republic of Sinowa fully recognises plural and multiple citizenships for standard workers and foreign nationals. Naturalising foreign nationals are no longer mandated to surrender their baseline external passports.

CRITICAL NOTICE: Holding dual or multiple nationalities is strictly prohibited for any individual seeking to hold public office, government positions, or state security clearances. Undivided national loyalty is the absolute baseline requirement for service to the State Council.

Clause II: Permanent Residency (PR) & Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)

For foreign nationals who do not immediately seek full multi-citizenship, the state provides a secure long-term settlement track:

  • Permanent Residency (PR) Card: Awarded to foreign industrial specialists under B-2 class frameworks who maintain three continuous years of faultless production records on the manufacturing grid.
  • Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR): Conferred upon passing standard security checks and demonstrating five continuous years of domestic residency. ILR status permanently exempts holders from foreign work permit caps.

Clause III: Public Infrastructure Entitlements

While full multiple-citizenship holders enjoy total un-tariffed state health insurance and tertiary schooling access, registered PR and ILR holders receive full public health coverage at heavily subsidised state-controlled rates, allowing complete integration into the national workforce network.
