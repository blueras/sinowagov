II. Social Credit System

Following State Council Directive 915, civic behaviours translate directly into an algorithmic scoring matrix. Positive contributions (exceeding industrial targets, completing volunteer shifts) improve scores. Infractions yield consequences including restricted transit and lower service priorities.
