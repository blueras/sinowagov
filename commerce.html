Ministry of Commerce & Technological Innovation

Regulating the private sector, managing Special Economic Zones (SEZs), and driving industrial growth under state guidance.

State-Guided Capitalism & Corporate Oversight

Sinowa supports a vibrant private sector to drive innovation. However, the State Council retains a "Golden Share" (1%) and a mandatory board seat in all major corporations. This ensures private profit aligns with the collective national interest.

Special Economic Zones (SEZs)

Foreign investors may operate within designated SEZs with reduced red tape, but must comply with labour and environmental standards. All SEZ transactions are monitored by the Central Bank.
