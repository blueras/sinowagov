Ministry of Defence & State Structure

The People's Republic of Sinowa operates under a dual-power administrative matrix, ensuring strategic ideological planning remains harmonised with daily state execution. The state infrastructure layout coordinates heavy construction divisions directly alongside military logistic cell commands.

⚔️ Conventional State Defence Mandate — Chapter 4

All citizens upon reaching the age of maturity (18 years) are legally conscripted into the Armed Forces of Sinowa for a strict 1-year mandatory service period. No exemptions are granted outside of prioritised medical infrastructure or industrial logistics assignments. Reserve obligations continue until age 45.

Structural Strategic Framework

The state infrastructure layout coordinates heavy construction divisions directly alongside military logistic cell commands. This central planning ensures that defensive capabilities scale directly with state economic manufacturing metrics across the collective realm.
