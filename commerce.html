Ministry of Commerce & Technological Innovation

Regulating the private sector, managing Special Economic Zones (SEZs), and driving industrial growth under state guidance.

State-Guided Capitalism

The State Council retains a "Golden Share" (1%) and a mandatory board seat in all major corporations. This ensures private profit never diverges from collective national interest.

2025 Performance: GDP growth 6.8% · Export growth 12.3% · 12,000 jobs created

Special Economic Zones

Three SEZs operate with streamlined regulations: Pearl River Delta Tech Hub (Tier 1), Northern Industrial Corridor (Tier 2), Western Development Zone (emerging). Foreign investment reached eK 2.3B in 2025.
