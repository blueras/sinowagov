National Education System

Cultivating human capital through absolute academic rigour and innovative assessment. Our goal: the world's best education system without sacrificing youth mental health.

I. The Sovereign State Grid (Free)

The backbone of Sinowan prosperity. State schools provide world-class, free education from nursery to university. The curriculum is a rigorous blend of STEM (based on real-world Chinese, German, and Singaporean content) and Humanities. It provides foundational discipline and intense academic depth to guarantee technical proficiency. Average class size: 24 · Student-teacher ratio: 18:1

II. International Academies (Private)

Fee-paying international schools are explicitly permitted for expatriates and citizens preferring alternative pedagogical styles (IB, British A-Levels, American AP). All private schools maintain international accreditation while adhering strictly to state security guidelines and mandatory civics education.

III. Sovereign Assessment Matrix (SAM)

To avoid a dystopian exam environment, we have abolished the stressful high-stakes final exam model. Instead, we combine best practices from around the world: continuous digital coursework logging (40%), practical vivas and collaborative labs (30%), and modular competency tests (30%). This produces highly adaptable workers without sacrificing youth mental health. University admission rate (2025): 68%

IV. Adult & Vocational Training

The State guarantees lifelong learning. All citizens receive an annual eK 2,000 training voucher. Over 1,200 vocational programmes are available via the SinowaLink app, covering everything from advanced manufacturing to digital design.
