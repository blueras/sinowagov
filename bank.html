Central Bank of Sinowa

Sovereign Issuing Authority · Supreme Monetary Policy Organ

The Central Bank holds an absolute monopoly over the issuance of legal tender. All e-Kwang (eK) currency is fully digital, backed by state production metrics.

I. Legal Tender & Digital e-Kwang Management

The e-Kwang is the sole legal tender. It is a stable, algorithmically managed digital currency. Every transaction is recorded on the state ledger to prevent fraud and capital flight. The Central Bank adjusts liquidity in real time to match industrial output.

II. Exchange Rate Policy

Sinowa maintains a managed float against major foreign currencies. The exchange rate is published daily by the Bureau of Statistics. Currency sync agreements with allied nations allow for seamless trade settlements.
