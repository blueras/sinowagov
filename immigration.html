Ministry of Home Affairs · Sovereign Registry

The Ministry of Home Affairs protects national documentation lines, civil status registration, and security resident records. Below are the official documents issued to all citizens and residents.

Biometric E-Passport

Integrated encrypted chips link citizens to secure account databases and public transit authorisation. Adult (18+): 10-year validity · Child (under 18): 3-year validity

Issuance fee
50 eK

National Identity Card

Mandatory for all citizens and residents aged 12+. Used for daily verification, factory shift allocation, border transit, and SinowaLink authentication.

Registration fee
30 eK

State Driving Licence

Eligible from age 16. Allows operation of private vehicles, agricultural machinery, and light commercial transport. Digital version available on SinowaLink.

Evaluation fee
20 eK

Citizenship & Residency

Dual Citizenship: Sinowa now permits dual citizenship for ordinary workers. However, public office holders, judges, and security personnel must hold sole Sinowan nationality.
Permanent Residency (PR): Awarded after 3 continuous years of faultless production records or 5 years of residency.
Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR): Conferred after 5 years of domestic residency + standard security checks. ILR holders receive public health coverage and minimum wage protections.
