National Education System

Cultivating human capital through rigorous academic standards and innovative assessment.

I. The Sovereign State Grid (Free)

State schools provide world-class free education. The curriculum blends STEM, humanities, and civic responsibility. All teachers are state-certified.

II. International Academies (Private)

Fee-paying international schools are permitted for expatriates and citizens who prefer alternative pedagogy, provided they adhere to state security guidelines.

III. Sovereign Assessment Matrix (SAM)

We have replaced high-pressure exams with continuous digital coursework logging, practical vivas and collaborative labs. This produces highly adaptable workers without harming mental health.
