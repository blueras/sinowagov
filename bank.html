Central Bank of Sinowa

Sovereign Issuing Authority · Supreme Organ of Monetary Policy

The Central Bank holds an absolute monopoly over the issuance of legal tender. All e-Kwang (eK) currency is fully digital, backed by state production metrics and managed through a secure blockchain ledger.

I. Legal Tender & Digital e-Kwang Management

The e-Kwang is the sole legal tender. It is a stable, algorithmically managed digital currency. Every transaction is recorded on the state ledger to prevent fraud, money laundering, and capital flight. The Central Bank adjusts liquidity in real time to match industrial output and inflation targets (2-3% annual).

Currency code: eK · Subunit: 1 eK = 100 fen · Total supply: Algorithmically capped at eK 50B

II. Exchange Rate Policy

Sinowa maintains a managed float against major foreign currencies (USD, EUR, GBP). The exchange rate is published daily at 09:00 local time by the Bureau of Statistics. Currency sync agreements with allied nations allow for seamless trade settlements with zero conversion fees.

III. Monetary Tools & Reserves

The Central Bank maintains foreign exchange reserves totalling approximately eK 12.8B. Key tools include: reserve requirements (currently 12%), open market operations, and the e-Kwang Liquidity Facility for state-owned enterprises.
