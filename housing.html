Urban Planning & Residential Allocation

Sinowa manages all land through a state-monopoly framework. Residential development is driven by efficient, high-density smart-city planning.

Property Leasehold Model

While the state retains land ownership, citizens possess 70-year transferable leaseholds. This system encourages robust property investment, personal wealth generation, and modern urban maintenance, ensuring cities remain clean, safe, and technologically advanced.

Civic Mobility & Hukou

The Municipal Residency framework manages urban infrastructure capacity. Your residency status (Hukou) determines your immediate access to local medical and educational nodes. Residency transitions are managed by the Home Affairs algorithm to prevent urban over-saturation.
