Ministry of Housing & Urban Development

Managing land monopolies, urban planning grids, and residential allocations.

I. 70-Year Leasehold Model

In accordance with socialist constitutional parameters, the State retains absolute ownership of 100% of all land. Citizens are permitted to purchase and trade 70-year residential leaseholds on state land. This balances robust private investment and modern urban maintenance while ensuring the government retains ultimate territorial sovereignty.

II. Municipal Integration (Hukou System)

The Municipal Residency framework manages urban infrastructure capacity. Your residency status (Hukou) determines your immediate access to local medical clinics and educational nodes. Residency transitions to Tier-1 megacities are algorithmically managed by the Ministry of Home Affairs to prevent urban over-saturation.
