Ministry of Commerce & Technological Innovation

The engine of the Sinowan Asian Century. The Ministry oversees the booming private sector, manages Special Economic Zones (SEZs), and ensures that capitalist innovation remains strictly guided by the national interests of the Vanguard Party.

Special Economic Zones (SEZs)

Designated coastal and industrial hubs where foreign direct investment (FDI) operates under hyper-capitalist efficiency. These zones drive the development of green-energy EV manufacturing, e-commerce networks, and high-tech software engineering, generating massive capital influxes for the sovereign treasury.

The "Golden Share" Protocol

While the private sector generates immense wealth, the State Council permanently retains a "1% Golden Share" and a mandatory board seat in all major corporate conglomerates. This ensures that tech giants and corporate monopolies never subvert national security or deviate from the Party's long-term economic plans.
