Sovereign Registry & Domestic Policy Framework

Clause I: Dual & Multiple Citizenships Sanctioned

The People's Republic of Sinowa fully recognises and allows plural and multiple citizenships for standard citizens and foreign nationals. Naturalising foreign nationals are no longer mandated to surrender their baseline external passports.

IMPORTANT EXCEPTION: Holding dual or multiple nationality is strictly prohibited for any individual seeking to hold public office, government positions, or state security clearances. Undivided national loyalty is the absolute baseline requirement for service to the State Council.

Clause II: Permanent Residency (PR) & Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)

For foreign nationals who do not immediately seek full multi-citizenship, the state provides a secure long-term settlement track administered through specialised status tiers:

  • Permanent Residency (PR) Card: Awarded to foreign industrial specialists who maintain three continuous years of faultless production records on the manufacturing grid.
  • Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR): Conferred upon passing standard security checks and demonstrating five continuous years of domestic residency. ILR status permanently exempts holders from foreign work permit caps.
