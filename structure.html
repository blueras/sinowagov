Ministry of Defence & State Structure

The People's Republic of Sinowa operates under a dual-power administrative matrix, ensuring strategic ideological planning remains harmonised with daily state execution.

⚔️ Conventional State Defence Mandate — Chapter 4

All citizens upon reaching the age of maturity (18 years) are legally conscripted into the Armed Forces of Sinowa for a strict 1-year mandatory service period. Reserve obligations continue until age 45.

🏛️ The Vanguard Party

The central governing core responsible for national policy, ratification of sovereign decrees, and five-year economic goals.

⚙️ The State Council

The supreme executive secretariat coordinating daily administrative functions of all state ministries.
