Sovereign Defensive Mandate

STATE CONVENTIONAL MANDATE: Under State Defense Law Chapter 4, all citizens upon reaching the age of maturity are legally conscripted into the Armed Forces of Sinowa for a strict 1-year mandatory service period. No exemptions are granted outside of prioritized medical infrastructure assignments.

Structural Framework

The state layout links industrial management teams directly with military supply cells, establishing that defensive preparedness scales synchronously with construction output.
