Ministry of Commerce & Technological Innovation

Regulating the private sector, managing Special Economic Zones (SEZs), and driving industrial growth.

State-Guided Capitalism & Corporate Oversight

Sinowa supports a booming, vibrant private sector to drive technological innovation and export growth. However, under our State-Guided framework, the State Council retains a "Golden Share" (1%) and a mandatory board seat in all major corporations. This ensures that private profit generation never diverges from the collective national interest.
