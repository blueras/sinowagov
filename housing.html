Ministry of Housing & Urban Development

Managing land monopolies, urban planning grids, and residential allocations. Our housing policy ensures affordable homes for all while maintaining state sovereignty over land.

I. 70-Year Leasehold Model

The State retains absolute ownership of 100% of all land. Citizens may purchase 70-year residential leaseholds. Average prices: Tier-1 city: eK 450k · Tier-2: eK 210k · Rural: eK 75k

II. Hukou Residency System

Your residency status determines access to local medical clinics and schooling. Tier-1 city transfers are algorithmically managed to prevent overcrowding. Currently 34% of urban households live in state-provided social housing.
