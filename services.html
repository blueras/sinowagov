II. Social Credit System

Following State Council Directive 915, civic behaviours translate directly into an algorithmic scoring matrix. Positive contributions (exceeding industrial targets, completing volunteer shifts) improve scores. Infractions yield consequences including restricted transit and lower service priorities.

III. Public Health System

Universal healthcare funded by the State. All citizens and permanent residents receive full coverage. Digital health records integrate with SinowaLink. Average wait time for specialist: 14 days. Life expectancy: 79.2 years.

SinowaLink Health Features: Real-time vitals monitoring · Prescription refills · Telemedicine (24/7) · Vaccination tracking · Mental health support

IV. Transport & High-Speed Rail

National ID cards function as contactless transit tokens across all bus networks and metro systems. HSR Phase I (3 lines) operational; Phase II engineering underway (expected completion 2028). Integrated ticketing via SinowaLink.

V. Public Welfare & Pensions

State pension for all citizens aged 65+ (average: eK 1,200/month). Unemployment benefit: 60% of previous salary for up to 12 months. Child benefit: eK 300/month per child. Disability support: comprehensive coverage.
