Housing & Urban-Rural Development

I. The Sovereign Land Monopoly & 70-Year Leaseholds

In accordance with socialist constitutional parameters, the State retains absolute ownership of 100% of all land within the People's Republic of Sinowa. However, to foster personal prosperity and wealth generation, citizens are permitted to purchase and trade 70-year residential leaseholds on state land. This creates a booming, highly lucrative property market while ensuring the government retains ultimate territorial sovereignty.

II. Municipal Integration (Hukou System)

To prevent unbalanced population density and ensure social services are perfectly allocated, all citizens are registered under a strict municipal residency framework. Access to local top-tier medical facilities and elite public schools is seamlessly linked to an individual's registered geographic node. Transferring residency to a Tier-1 megacity requires a high Social Credit score and targeted industrial sector approval.
