Sovereign Registry & Citizenship

The Ministry of Home Affairs manages national documentation and residency tracking.

Biometric Documents

E-Passport: 50 eK (valid 10 years for adults, 3 years for children).
National ID Card: 30 eK – mandatory for all citizens.
Driving Licence: 20 eK – eligible from age 16.

Dual Citizenship & Residency

Sinowa now permits dual citizenship for ordinary workers. However, public office holders must hold sole Sinowan nationality. Permanent Residency (PR) is available after 3 years of faultless production records; Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR) after 5 years.
