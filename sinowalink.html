Mandatory Registration

All citizens and permanent residents are required by law to register on SinowaLink within 30 days of turning 12 (or upon receiving residency). Visitors may use a limited guest version without full civic features.
Security & Privacy: All data is encrypted and stored on sovereign servers. Biometric authentication optional but recommended. Your data is never sold to third parties.

SinowaLink Kids — Parental Controls

Parents can limit screen time, restrict social features, approve contacts, and monitor activity. The Kids version includes the full school curriculum portal where homework assignments and grades are automatically synced from the National Education System.
