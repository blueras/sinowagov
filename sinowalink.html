Mandatory Registration

All citizens and permanent residents are required by law to register on SinowaLink within 30 days of turning 12 or upon receiving residency. Visitors may use a limited guest version without full civic features. Registration requires biometric verification at any Home Affairs office.
Security and Privacy: All data is encrypted and stored on sovereign servers within Sinowan territory. Biometric authentication is optional but recommended for full functionality. Your data is never sold to third parties and is protected under State Privacy Law Chapter 12.

SinowaLink Kids — Parental Controls

Parents can limit screen time, restrict social features, approve contacts, and monitor activity through the Family Link dashboard. The Kids version includes the full school curriculum portal where homework assignments and grades are automatically synced from the National Education System.
